# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Dashboardy serves a mixed audience:

- External customer admins managing their organization/workspace, access, and reporting surfaces.
- Internal operators/analysts using the product to monitor outcomes, validate data quality, and support customers.

Users typically operate in focused work sessions, often returning to the same core views repeatedly.

## Product Purpose

Dashboardy is a dashboard and reporting product backed by an API, designed to help users:

- Monitor key metrics and system or business health.
- Explore data through filtering and drilldowns to answer specific questions.
- Deliver insights via shareable or exportable reporting outputs.

Success looks like users finding the right answer quickly, trusting what they see, and reliably producing reports without friction or ambiguity.

## Brand Personality

- Calm
- Confident
- Premium

The UI should feel steady and intentional, emphasizing clarity and trust over decoration.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS template aesthetics and “dashboard starter kit” sameness.
- Neon-on-black, high-chroma “crypto dashboard” styling.
- Spreadsheet-dense, intimidating screens that force cognition before context.

## Design Principles

- Reduce cognitive load before adding features.
- Prioritize trustworthiness: make data provenance, scope, and freshness easy to understand.
- Make complex exploration feel guided and reversible.
- Optimize for repeat use: fast scanning, predictable navigation, and sensible defaults.
- Keep the interface calm under pressure, including during incidents or deadlines.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.2 AA.
- Support reduced motion preferences and avoid relying on color alone for meaning.
